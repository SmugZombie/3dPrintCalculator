About

This is a free, open-source, no-signup 3D print cost calculator inspired by commercial tools in this space. All "pro" features — unlimited quotes, custom filament/printer libraries, multi-material, batch production and stock tracking — are unlocked and free, with no account required. Your data never leaves your device.

Free & open source · MIT licensed · no tracking, no signup, no cloud — everything runs and stays in your browser.
